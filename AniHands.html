Anime Hands

Stage 1 - Basic shape

Anime is 2d so instead of Draw a 3d box like in the traditional hand tutorial. As you can see in the picture you need to draw a shape with 5 different sides. One of the sides is for the fingers another is for the wrist and the other is for the root of the thumbs

Stage 2

The second stage is basically just connecting the wrist to the hand you start with the circle for basically gaging where to start the wrist.

Stage 3

In this stage you a are going to start on the fingers. Draw a single line of the thumb side to gauge where to start the thumb. And draw a trapizoid on the fingers side with line going through it to show were the knuckles ore going to be.

Stage 4

in this stage your going to you are going to draw three verticle lines for the knuckles. you are also going to curve out some of the lines, and you are also going to extend the thumb line.

Stage 5

In this stage we are going to finish up everything we start by rounding out the finger and seperating them and finishing up the thumb. and erasing all the guide lines, like the wrist and the knuckle lines
