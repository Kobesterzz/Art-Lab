Traditional Head

Stage 1 - Circle

In this first stage it may seem simple but you need to figure out where you want to start the head and the size of the head.

Stage 2 - Arrows

you can skip this stage if you want but i typically draw arrow in the direction i want the face to look so it easier for me to under stand what you will need to see.

Stage 3 - The side of the head

In this stage you are going to draw a cirle on the side of the circle that you are going to see the ear on.

Stage 4 - Face Guide

In this stage you are going to draw guidline on the front of the face to show where the eye brows and the eyes will go. Aswell as the mouth and the nose.

Stage 5 - Chin

Depending the sive of the head will determine how long of a line you will draw in this stage. this line is the guide to show you where to draw the chin.

Stage 6 - Jaw pt.1

In this stage you are going to start the jaw. but this line is going to be right below the ear so make sure that it is lined up to where you want it to be.

Stage 7 - Jaw pt.2

In this second part of the jaw is going to be a lond diagnol line between the chin and the other peice of the jaw for the main jaw bone.

Stage 8 - Eye Guide

In this part you are going to draw the eye guid on the face. to start right under the eyebrow line you are going to draw a simmilar line but push a bit closer to the ear. arfter that you are going to draw two diagnol lines to connect these two guid lines.

Stage 9 - The Cheeks

In this step you are going to draw the cheek bones. start with a curved line connecting the chin to the eye. And another curved line on the opposite side.

Stage 10 - The Neck

In the middle of the chin draw a line going down for the front of the neck. And on the back side of the head draw a longer line for the back of the neck. and connect it at the bottom.
