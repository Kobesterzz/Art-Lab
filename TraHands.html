Traditional Hands

Stage 1 - Box Method

In this method you are going to draw a 3d shape as seen in the picture. Depending on the POV (point of view) you may see more than one side or you may just see one side.

(You can see this in the Picture)

Stage 2 - The Thumb

In this stage we are going to Add the thumb to the hand. You are going to start out by drawing an egg looking oval on the thumb side of the hand. Just like a snow man you are going to draw two smaller shape above it. For the last part you are going to draw the tip of the thumb and for this you are going to draw the same shape but a bit longer and one side is curved.

(You can see this in the Picture)

Stage 3 - The Mitten

this stage is called the mitten because we start drawing the finger off simple. we are going to group the fingers together in a formation that looks like a mitten. Your are going to group the first three fingers and leave the pointer and the thumb on its own.

(You can see this in the Picture)

Stage 4 - Connect

In this stage you are going to connect every thing and smooth it all out. Use the mitten a a guide for the fingers. at the end erase any guid lines you used.

(You can see this in the Picture)

Shading

Stage 1 - lights

In the first part of shading you wanna start out with the light areas And work your way to the darks. You can start by going over some areas smoothly to show that those parts of the hand are a bit darker EX: parts of the hand facing away from the sun but still getting a bit of sunlight.

(You can see this in the Picture)

Stage 2 - Darks

In this stage we are going to go over the darker areas. these are the parts of the hand that really dont get any sunlight. be sure to be subtle with it. Remember that art is a process and its not going to be easy from the start. Practice Makes Progress!!
